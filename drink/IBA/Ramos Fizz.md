# Ramos Fizz

## Variations

### IBA

* **45 ml** Gin
* **15 ml** Lime juice
* **15 ml** Fresh lemon juice
* **30 ml** Sugar syrup
* **60 ml** Cream
* **1** Egg white
* **3 dashes** Orange flower water
* **2 drops** Vanilla extract
* Soda water

Pour all ingredients (except soda) in a mixing glass, dry shake (no ice) for two minutes, add ice and hard shake for another minute.

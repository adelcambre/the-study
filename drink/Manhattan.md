# Manhattan

## Variations

### IBA

* **50 ml** rye whiskey (*(ri)1*)
* **20 ml** sweet red vermouth (*Punt e Mes*)
* **1 dash** *Angostura Bitters*
* Maraschino cherry

### Bourbon

Substitute rye whiskey for bourbon whiskey.

### Perfect

Add equal parts whiskey and vermouth.
